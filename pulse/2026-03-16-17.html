The Real AI Opportunity Is Making SAP Usable

Enterprise AI's biggest impact won't be shiny new products — it'll be wrapping legacy systems like SAP, Salesforce, and ServiceNow with interfaces that don't require a PhD. Key stat: upgrading from SAP ECC to S4HANA costs $700M, takes 3 years, and requires 50 consultants. AI can shortcut all of that with "computer-use" agents that navigate the 30-40% of workflows without APIs.
