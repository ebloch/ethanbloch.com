Hiro

April 8, 2025

Back in 2015 when I launched Digit, I dreamed of creating a financial AI, but the technology wasn't ready. Digit helped millions of people save billions of dollars but it never became a financial AI. It turns out the dream never died — it just had to wait.

After leaving Digit in 2023, I had no plans to start another company. I needed to recharge.

However one of my partners from Digit, Rushabh Doshi, immediately went off exploring and building with LLMs; ChatGPT had just launched a few months prior.

In early 2024, Rushabh showed me what he had been building and learning. I was shook. The universe was calling. We had to go see if the dream of a financial AI was now possible.

After 12 months of exploring the frontier of LLMs + financial data we believe it is.

We have entered the era of personal financial intelligence.

The computer started as a tool you used at work, until Apple made it personal; "a computer in every home." The CFO started as a job at work, and Hiro is making it personal; a CFO in every pocket.

What does this mean? It means no more app-hopping between Google searches, spreadsheets, budgeting tools, banking apps and investment accounts. It means having a super sharp financial partner that knows your situation deeply and can actually help you make better decisions. It means the end of financial advice that sounds smart but isn't personalized to your reality.


We've been testing with friends and family for the past 6 months. Starting today, we're rolling out a larger private beta.
